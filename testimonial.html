Featured Case Study

Solving Cash Flow and Compliance Challenges for a Singapore SME

Lumina Design Pte Ltd

Interior Design and Renovation

SME (25 Employees)

06 June 2024

Problem

Lumina Design, a growing interior design firm, faced two major challenges that impacted its growth and operational efficiency.

Irregular Cash Flow
Delayed Payments

The firm often dealt with delayed payments from clients due to its project-based nature. This inconsistency in cash flow made it difficult to cover expenses, including payroll, supplier payments, and rent.

No Clear Forecasting System

Difficult to track incoming and outgoing payments, leading to poor financial planning.

Outdated Financial Reporting
Struggles in Management of Regulatory and Tax Obligations

Lumina Design’s finance team lacked the knowledge to efficiently manage GST filings and comply with Inland Revenue Authority of Singapore (IRAS) regulations.

Penalties incurred

Lumina Design faced delays in filing their GST returns and incurred penalties from tax authorities

Solution
Cash Flow Optimisation
Automated Invoicing

Implemented a cloud-based system to automate invoicing and payment reminders, speeding up client payments.

Cash Flow Forecasting

Developed forecasting tools to help GreenLeaf predict future cash inflows and manage expenses better.

Updated Financial Reporting
Real Time Monitoring

Migrated manual accounting to cloud-based software (Xero), automating financial reports and providing real-time insights into their cash flow and profitability.

25% faster invoice payments improved liquidity.

Tax Compliance
Automated GST returns submission

Ensures timely and accurate filings to avoid penalties.

Strategic tax planning

Leverage deductions and incentives, reducing GreenLeaf’s tax liability.

Tax savings and compliance eliminated penalties and reduced tax burden by 12%.
